### PRODUCT & TECHNOLOGY

#### Q: What exactly does Call Helm do?
**A:** Call Helm is a white-label voice services platform that transforms any business into a sophisticated call center in 5 minutes. We provide instant phone number provisioning, AI-powered call scripts, real-time monitoring, and complete call center infrastructure at 75% less cost than traditional solutions.

#### Q: How is this different from Twilio or other APIs?
**A:** Twilio provides raw infrastructure APIs that require significant development. Call Helm is a complete, ready-to-use application with UI, AI integration, user management, billing, analytics, and white-label capabilities. Think of Twilio as AWS and Call Helm as Shopify - we've built the application layer that businesses actually need.

#### Q: What's your technical moat?
**A:** Our moat consists of:
1. **AI Integration**: Deep GPT-4 integration for script generation and call analysis
2. **Real-time Architecture**: WebSocket-based live monitoring unique in SMB market
3. **Multi-provider Abstraction**: Seamlessly switch between carriers without code changes
4. **White-label Infrastructure**: Complete rebranding capability including domains
5. **Compliance Built-in**: TCPA, GDPR, HIPAA-ready architecture

#### Q: How difficult would it be for competitors to replicate?
**A:** While the basic features could be replicated in 6-12 months, our advantages are:
- 18-month head start in AI training data
- Established partner network creating lock-in
- Direct carrier relationships taking months to establish
- Complex real-time infrastructure requiring specialized expertise
- Network effects from marketplace and integrations

#### Q: What happens if OpenAI raises prices or becomes unavailable?
**A:** We've built provider abstraction allowing us to switch between OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, and open-source models. Our AI costs are only 5% of revenue, so even a 10x increase would be manageable. We're also fine-tuning smaller models for specific tasks to reduce dependence.

#### Q: Why hasn't someone built this before?
